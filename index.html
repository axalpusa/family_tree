Family Comparisons & Relationships

Similarities in My Family

My parents are married for many years. My mother and grandmother both love cooking. My sisters and I all value education. Eyllin, Diego and I are all in our 20s and 30s. Ashlly and I are both studying right now.

My father and brother both enjoy outdoor activities. My mother and Ashlly are both very organized people. My grandmother and Eyllin are both mothers. All of us love family gatherings.

Differences in My Family
